This is
SARA NEGRI

Sara Negri is an international student from Italy pursuing her second Master's degree at Old Dominion University.

She grew up surrounded by figures who taught her the values of loyalty, work ethic, and hard work.

In 2017 she got her Bachelor's degree in Political Science and Foreign Affairs at the LUISS Guido Carli University in Rome, Italy.

The same year she moved to Norfolk, Virginia, to pursue her Master's in Economics at Old Dominion University.

During her academic journey, she discovered her passion for Business Analytics, which encouraged her to complete her Analytics and Big Data certificate and develop her technical skills, including software and programming languages. She obtained her Masters degree in May 2021, and a few months after, she started her second Master in Data Science and Analytics at the same university.

Sara is a proactive student who likes working in teams, exploring new opportunities, and facing challenges with success.

She likes leaving her sign to the community participating in volunteering activities, and helping people in need.

In addition, she enjoys traveling, meeting new people with different backgrounds, and capturing wonderful landscapes with her camera .
